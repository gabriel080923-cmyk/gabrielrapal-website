About Me

Hello! I’m Gabriel Rapal, an 18-year-old passionate about boxing, born on September 8, 2007. I am currently an amateur boxer with a record of 10 wins, 5 losses, and 1 draw, competing in the 50–53 kg weight category. I have been training in boxing for three years, starting around April 2023, and this year my goal is to turn professional and take my boxing career to the next level.

My Journey

Year 2023

"Starting from scratch this year, I stepped into the ring and earned 3 wins, 1 loss, and 1 draw as a beginner. Every match taught me the basics of boxing, from footwork to defense, and helped me discover my passion for the sport. It was a year of learning, pushing my limits, and laying the foundation for my boxing journey."

Year 2024

"I fought 4 matches and finished the year with a 3-1 record, learning and growing with every fight. In 2025, I continued training and competing, ending the year with a 6-2-1 record. Each match, win, loss, or draw has shaped me, and I’m just getting started on this path to becoming stronger and better in the ring."

Year 2025

"2025 was one of my toughest years, testing me both mentally and physically due to events outside the ring. Despite the challenges, I fought hard and ended the year with a 3-2 record, bringing my overall career record to 10-4-1. Every fight, win or loss, has shaped me, and I continue to grow stronger with each match."
